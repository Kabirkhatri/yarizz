Our Vibe

More Than Just Food

Yaarizz was born from a simple idea: that good food is best shared with good friends (Yaars). We specialize in authentic Indian street food and snacks that bring back the nostalgic flavors of college breaks and rainy evenings.

Our mission is to serve fresh, hygienic, and flavorful snacks like Vadapav, Breadpakoda, and Frankie, accompanied by a hot cup of Masala Chai. We use only the finest ingredients and traditional recipes to ensure every bite takes you back home.
